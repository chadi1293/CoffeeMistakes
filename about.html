About CoffeeMistakes.com

We're on a mission to help coffee lovers make smarter equipment choices. No fluff, no bias, just honest advice built from 1000+ real Reddit discussions.

Our Story

CoffeeMistakes.com was born from frustration. We watched too many friends waste hundreds of dollars on coffee equipment that didn't deliver. A blade grinder paired with a $1000 espresso machine. Pre-ground coffee with a $500 brewer. Sound familiar?

So we did something different: we built a tool based on actual coffee community wisdom. We analyzed thousands of discussions from r/Coffee, coffee forums, and real user experiences. The result? A simple quiz that cuts through the noise and delivers personalized recommendations that actually work.

This site is free, forever. No paywall, no email capture, no manipulative design. Just a resource that genuinely helps you avoid expensive mistakes.

How We Built This

1. Community Analysis

We spent months analyzing 1000+ discussions from r/Coffee, coffee forums, and enthusiast communities. The goal: identify patterns in what equipment works and what doesn't.

2. Expert Consensus

We filtered recommendations through the lens of actual experts—people who've invested years in coffee brewing, not just marketing departments. We ignored outliers and focused on widely-agreed truths.

3. Budget Realism

We created pathways for every budget level, from $85 setups to $1000+ investments. You don't need expensive gear to make excellent coffee. We prove it.

4. The Quiz

We condensed years of collective wisdom into 8 simple questions. The quiz doesn't judge—it just matches your situation to the best path forward.

Funding & Business Model

CoffeeMistakes.com is completely free and always will be. Here's how we sustain it:

Amazon Associate Commissions

When you click our Amazon links and make a purchase, we earn a small commission (1-10%). This covers hosting costs and keeps the site running. You pay nothing extra—Amazon prices are identical whether you use our link or not.

No Ads

We could plaster this site with ads. We don't. The user experience is more important than ad revenue.

No Tracking

We don't sell your data or use aggressive tracking. That would generate revenue but violate your privacy.

Our Promise

If our funding model ever conflicts with your interests, we'll find another way. Your trust is more valuable than any commission.
